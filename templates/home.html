AI Video Dubbing

This project focuses on English to Hindi video dubbing using advanced AI technology. The AI model ensures accurate lip-syncing between the original video and its dubbed Hindi version.

Get Started

How Wave2Lip Dubbing Works

Our AI-powered system, built using the Wave2Lip model, focuses on English to Hindi dubbing while maintaining perfect lip-syncing.

It analyzes the facial movements and speech of the original video, generating a lip-synced Hindi version.
